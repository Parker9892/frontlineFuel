AUTOMATIC

Levco uses a proprietary system to calculate and schedule the appropriate time for your next oil delivery. Automatic delivery is perfect for business owners and property managers who never want to worry about their oil tank running dry or having to call for rush deliveries. Based on a number of factors including your delivery history, the size of your tanks, and the weather, Levco is able to schedule and make timely deliveries to your property.
